ABOUT US

We Want You To Get The Most Out Of Your Business.

Why LUX Salon Suites?

Whether you’re a beauty or wellness professional, you probably entered into the industry to help people look and feel their best, and you wanted to have the freedom to do it your way - not within the confines of someone else's vision. Over time, working for someone else can decrease your drive, as well as your ability to be fulfilled. Before you know it, you begin to lose your passion and wonder where it all went awry...

At LUX Salon Suites, we focus on finding the best ways to support you and your dream, so you have the freedom to focus on your passion and on building a business that is truly yours.

We provide beautiful, modern, state-of-the-art facilities where you can be proud to host your clients.  Each private suite comes equipped with smart locks, window coverings, and all the basic equipment you need to get started.  If you have a bigger vision, our contractors can work with you to customize your space to best meet your needs and reflect your brand. 

Each shop owner at LUX Salon Suites is supported by our entire team. We take some of the mundane and time-consuming tasks off your plate, such as washing towels, so you can spend time doing what you love and serving your clients. Our model is also focused on building a supportive business owner network to facilitate greater opportunities for referrals and collaborations within the LUX community. We believe that by building each other up, everyone will achieve greater success.

At LUX, you get all the benefits of having your own salon business without the drawbacks of being on your own!
